Privacy

What we collect, and what we don't.

Last updated June 24, 2026

studiwize is in development. Right now the only thing this site does is collect an email address for the launch waitlist. This page explains exactly what that means.

What we collect

One thing: the email address you type into the waitlist form. That's it. We don't ask for your name, your school, or anything else, and there's no account to create.

Why we collect it

So we can send you one email when the studiwize iOS app is ready to download. That's the only reason your address is on the list.

Who handles it

Waitlist submissions are processed by Formspree, the form service that receives and stores the signups on our behalf. Your email is kept there and is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or other third parties.

Cookies and tracking

The landing page sets no advertising cookies and does not sell your data. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which means your browser requests files from Google's servers when the page loads.

Getting removed

You can be taken off the list at any time — just email hello@studiwize.com and we'll delete your address. When the app launches and the waitlist has done its job, the list is retired.

Changes

If this policy changes as studiwize grows into a full product, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about any of this? hello@studiwize.com.
