Questions

The things people ask first.

What files can I upload?

PDF or EPUB — the formats most textbooks and course readers come in. You bring a file you already own; there's no catalog to buy from.

Is it okay to upload my textbook?

studiwize is built for files you own — your purchased textbooks, your course PDFs, your own documents. It turns them into audio and a study guide for your personal study. It's not a way to share or redistribute copyrighted material.

Does it really read the whole book?

Yes. It narrates the actual text you uploaded, chapter by chapter, in order — not a summary of it. The study guide is built on top so you can review after you listen.

Does it work offline?

Once a book is built, the audio is yours on the device — no signal needed on the train, the plane, or the dead zone on your commute.
